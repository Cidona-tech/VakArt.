About us

A complete guide on how to bring your imagination to life. Through our step-by-step videos, you'll be able to create any possible crafts you desire- be it baskets, mats, hats or bags. And guess what? After acquiring the appropriate skills and techniques here, you'll be able to weave your very own and unique designs.

After learning how to weave through our videos, you will be able to create your very own design. What does this imply? You won't have to spend a large amount of money on new bags/hats or any other related things, for you will have the ability to make it yourself. You will also contribute in reducing our carbon footprint. As the manufactured items are a 100% biodegradable, long lasting and very sturdy, you won't need to purchase plastic bags anymore. Furthermore, you may also start a new business through this, thus providing yourself with the opportunity of becoming self-employed and a way of earning money.
